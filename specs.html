Best Creative Practices

These recommendations are meant to provide guidelines for creating your visual to achieve the best possible results. If you have any questions, we can provide assistance as well as production services.

General information
for best results

Avoid thin fonts that can become difficult to read Less is often more effective for conveying a clear message and branding No audio : message clear and concise, avoid existing videos with people talking Bright colours and good contrast attract attention and make text easier to read.

Digital Horizontal
2-screen configuration

Option 2

Second option is playing the same content on both screens however less impactful but does not require custom

Option 1

Best usage is a confluent design that uses the panoramic setup to have design that travels from one screento the other

Important that text remain centered on each screen and not travel between
