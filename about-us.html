our company

NEO (neo-ooh.com) is a Canadian advertising network established in 430 sites, 150 municipalities and has 3,000+ ad displays nationwide in shopping malls, fitness centers, C-stores, and gas stations.

NEO’s network consists of digital displays, static posters, spectacular formats, and speciality products, including technologies allowing us to broadcast advertising campaigns on smart phones, at the local and national level.

NEO is wholly owned by Media Group which also owns Speed-XM (speed-xm.com) and STAFF (staffpersonnel.com), Eastern Canada’s leading experiential marketing and event staffing agencies.
